OUR STORY

Founded on Integrity, Built for the Future.

Founded by Lucky Ngolothi in 2017, NGOJO Project Management began with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern engineering efficiency.

Over the years, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary powerhouse, delivering high-stakes commercial projects and refined residential solutions across the region. Our commitment to South Africa's growth is reflected in every structure we build and every circuit we wire.
