The Legacy of Lucky Ngolothi

Founded in 2017 by Lucky Ngolothi, NGOJO Project Management was born from a vision of bringing rigorous electrical engineering standards to large-scale project execution.

After qualifying in Electrical Engineering, Lucky recognized a critical gap in the market: the need for project management that treats every structural decision with the precision of a high-voltage circuit. What started as a focused technical consultancy has evolved into a multi-disciplinary powerhouse.

Today, we are more than just contractors; we are partners in progress, ensuring that every foundation we lay and every wire we pull contributes to a safer, more reliable infrastructure for all South Africans.
